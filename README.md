DukanBook: A Local Credit Management Web App for Mumbai Shopkeepers"*

Introduction:
In Mumbai’s vibrant local markets — from dry fish sellers to general kirana shops — the *“Udhaar” (credit) system* is an essential part of daily transactions. However, most shopkeepers still rely on *manual notebooks*, leading to lost entries, missed payments, and lack of clarity.
This project proposes *UdhaarBook, a **simple, multilingual, mobile-friendly web application* that allows small shopkeepers to *digitally manage their daily credit transactions* in a familiar, easy-to-use format without requiring any technical knowledge.


Problem Statement:
Despite being a major commercial city, *90%+ of Mumbai’s small vendors* (especially in traditional markets) do not use any digital tool to manage credit transactions. Manual entries lead to:
* Lost records
* Confusion in settlement
* Dependency on memory
* No customer communication for reminders

There’s a gap for a *low-tech, localized, offline-friendly, and language-accessible* credit management tool.
